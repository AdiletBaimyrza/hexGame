## Hex Game

A **two-player board game** in which players must connect opposite sides of a game board to win. It is played on an 11Ã—11 board The **Red player** must connect upper side with the bottom side, while the **Blue player** must connect the left side with the right side. **A player wins when they successfully connect their sides together through a chain of adjacent same-color hexes.** Draws are impossible due to the topology of the game board.
